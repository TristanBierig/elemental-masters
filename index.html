What it's about

Kill enemies by jumping on their heads or strike them
with your basic Attack [ Q ].
To cast your Spells you need to collect "Mana" from
fallen enemies.
Keep in mind: Spells are more powerful and can hit
and kill multiple Targets at once.
Collect Stars to transform and unleash the true power
of the Elements!
